About

My name is Tyler S. Morren, and I am a Web Developer. I originally went to school for Civil Engineering, but during my last semester, I realized I wanted to do something different. So I started teaching myself the basics of Web Development while finishing school. I learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through Khan Academy and loved it.

Two days after I graduated with my Civil Engineering degree, I was back in school for Web Development. Since then, I have received a Web Development Certificate and have continued to learn as much as I can about the field. In school I learned about PHP, MySQL, jQuery, Bootstrap, and WordPress. In my free time, I learn about emerging web technologies and diversify my skills.

As a professional I've gained a lot of experience by working on numerous consumer facing SaaS applications. I have experience with both Frontend and Backend development. For backend development, I primarily use Laravel, and for frontend I primarily use Angular4, although it varies by project.

Outside of development, I'm a huge basketball fan, like to go hiking, and enjoy playing video games with my friends!

If you want to know more, email me at tmorren1@gmail.com, or follow me on GitHub (tmorren) or LinkedIn!
